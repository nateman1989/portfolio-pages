OKC ZOO EXHIBITS

BEARS

Currently, the Zoo has two species of bears--grizzly bears and black bears. Their exhibits are located in the Oklahoma Trails area of the Zoo. Guests always enjoy visiting grizzly Zoo-favorites, Will and Wiley, who can be seen in the Cross Timbers section of Oklahoma Trails!

CAT FOREST

Some have spots. Some have stripes. And some have a little of both. In Cat Forest, you'll get to visit a wide variety of cat carnivores--from snow leopards and jaguars to tigers and bobcats. Cat Forest is an enjoyable maze of forest-like trails and several buildings with glass enclosures for year-round observation.

CHILDREN'S ZOO

The Children's Zoo opened on March 12, 2010, as a 2.5 acre area within the Oklahoma City Zoo dedicated to the enrichment and education of children. Children can explore, touch and immerse themselves in a natural outdoor environment that will stimulate their imagination, encourage exploration and give them a greater appreciation for nature.

ELEPHANT HERD

The elephant family in our care is special to us and the world. Asian elephants can grow up to 11,000 pounds and stand about 10 feet tall, although females elephants generally grow to about 8 ½ feet tall and weigh less than their male counterparts.

HERPETARIUM

With more than 65 habitats, you will find slithering, scaly, shiny and sleek animals to fascinate you and enhance your learning experience at the Herpetarium. Built in 1928, the historic building has recently undergone renovations to highlight avibrant diversity of reptiles and amphibians from around the world.

GREAT ESCAPE

Step into the world of tropical beauty and experience the habitat of the great apes - Great EscApe. Venture into the world of the great apes at Great EscApe, a world-class exhibit that allows adventurers to journey through a rainforest and view gorillas, orangutans and chimpanzees.

HOOFED ANIMALS

The Zoo's collection includes a herd of majestic giraffes. Who knew that hoofed animals in the Zoo's Savannah exhibit could cause such a stir?The exhibit also includes some African carnivore, such as the wild dog, cheetah and bat-eared fox.

POWERFUL PACHYDERM

Guests can also explore the unique outdoor yards with water features, mud wallows and grassland. These yards enable Zoo keepers to shift the rhinos from one grazing and browsing area to another, enhancing their enrichment opportunities.

ISLAND LIFE

Showcasing animals from around the world that live on islands, your tour through the Island Life exhibit is an island hopping experience. You can see the Abaco Island boa constrictor and the robust San Esteban Island chuckwalla lizard in habitats that mimic their natural island existence.

LION OVERLOOK

There is a new king in town! African lion, Hubert, joined the Zoo in late 2012. Like Aslan before, Hubert joined the pride as a healthy, young and handsome cat.

MARINE MAMMALS

By far one of the Zoo's most popular attractions, the marine mammal showcase at the Noble Aquatic Center is a spectacular showcase of California Sea Lions and Harbor Seals. It's an experience where understanding and education go hand-in-hand with entertainment and family fun.
