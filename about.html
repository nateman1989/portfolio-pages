Safari Cart Rentals

Enjoy a guided, two-hour tour of the Zoo by renting a Safari Cart. Full payment must accompany any reservation, the cost is $250. Tours are given at 10 a.m., 1 p.m. and 3 p.m. Due to cart size, certain areas must be walked on foot. Reserve by emailing visitorservices@okczoo.org or calling 405-425-0262.

Zoo Concessions

The Zoo offers a wide variety of eateries operated by our merchandising partner, Lancer Hospitality. The Canopy restaurant at the OKC Zoo offers a delectable dining experience in a rainforest atmosphere with outside patio option.

Zoo Gift Shops

The Zoo has five unique gift shops - Safari Gift Shop, General Store, and Lion's Lair. Each store, operated by our food, beverage and merchandising partner Lancer Hospitality, offers unique gifts for all ages.
